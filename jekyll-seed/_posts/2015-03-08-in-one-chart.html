Information Design is playing an increasingly critical role in everyday journalism. The movement from word and picture to “words within diagrams” is building a new form of truth-telling and storytelling — and with it, a new journalistic aesthetic.

Lupi, "The Architecture of a Data Visualization"; Feb 26, 2015

A "movement from word and picture to 'words within diagrams'" in journalism means a movement for both journalists and readers. For jouranlists, it's the movement towards a better mapping between information and their medium. For readers, it's a movement toward learning the heuristics to discern visual, fuzzy truths from visual, damn lies.
