Androgynous Names

A visualization of US census data showing names that have been historically androgynous.

Under the hood

Discussion

Visualizing the US Census' baby name database is almost a rite of passage for data visualizers. The dataset is already cleaned. Baby names are a familiar concept. I appreciate the rituals in life; thus this visualization!

Although clean, many years of the baby names database are incorrect. By analogy, it is precise but inaccurate.
